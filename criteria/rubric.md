# Rubric for Creative Work Assessment

## Technical Requirements (Pass/Fail)
1. Word count — minimum 100 words
2. Font name — must be Verdana or Arial throughout
3. Font size — must be 12 or 14 pt throughout
4. No long dashes — em dashes (—) and en dashes (–) are not permitted

## Content Quality (1–5 each, total 25 points)
1. Relevance (1–5) — Does the work address the assigned topic directly and meaningfully?
2. Depth of thought (1–5) — Quality of reasoning, analysis, and original thinking.
3. Structure (1–5) — Logical flow, clear organization, coherent introduction/body/conclusion.
4. Originality (1–5) — Personal voice, unique perspective, avoidance of generic phrases.
5. Language quality (1–5) — Clarity, vocabulary, grammar, and fluency.
